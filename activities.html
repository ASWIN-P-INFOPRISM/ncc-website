Freedom Awarness Marathon

Azadi Ka Amrith Mahothsav

We NCC cadets of MAR DIONYSIUS COLLEGE PAZHANJI interacted with freedom Awareness Run team of navy. The team started running at 5:30am from Thrissur and reached at 1:30pm in our college. They altmost covered 40 km before reaching the college Our NCC cadets welcomed them and arranged refreshment drinks and beds for their hospitality and also a small token of appreciation were gifted for runners. NCC caretaker Anisha ramadas , SUO. Sreerag P Monitored the programme.

Dress Distribution Campaign

Avarum Udukkatte

In this pandemic situation we NCC cadets of MAR DIONYSIUS COLLEGE PAZHANJI distribute ONAKOODI to the suffering people . In conjunction with Onam festival we NCC boys unit of MAR DIONYSIUS COLLEGE conduct a programme "AVARUM UDUKATTE" were successfully finished. The last few weeks before Onam we collected some new clothes from Kunnamkulam, Guruvayoor, Wadakkanchery and Perumpilavu and we distribute this clothes to the poor background people who is suffering from this pandemic situation in Guruvayoor, Porkulam and Changaramkulam. For this program we got appreciations from directorate level and the program was telecasted in dd news by DDG. Brig. P. K. Sunilkumar.

Blood Donors Directory

Claret 2021

We NCC SD UNIT of MAR DIONYSIUS COLLEGE PAZHANJI BLOOD DONORS DIRECTORY released as “CLARET” on the occasion of World Blood Donor Day in order to help people who wants blood in urgent. For this we collected the data like contact number of the persons who are willing to donate their blood and stored it. Those who are in urgent for blood can check this data and call these persons.

Kargil Vijay Diwas

A Talk With Kargil Hero

In conjunction with July 26, Kargil Vijay Divas the NCC boys unit of Mar Dionysius College conduct a talk session with a Ex-HVL Mr.Shajan. The programme was conducted at this residence The importance of this session is to show the value of every soilder in our society. And how they are treated after their retirements. The session is planned in a way of sharing his life experiences, about his retirement.He is a part of Kargil war so he can tell more about the war. He also share his valuable time for us and also share his all experience in this army life. Especially at the of kargil war time . It was a valuable talk session with him and we got many valuable lesson from his experience. After that session our mind were changed and give more respect towards the soldier .from his experience we know that all the soldier were doing service to their family but also for the entire people of the country and that session our respect towards the soldier were increased and give a special place in heart also we respect the more others. It was a informative session with him and also clarify all the doubt about the services SUO. Sreerag, SGT. Vishnu, CDT. Afsal , CDT. Gautam. led the way of the programme.
